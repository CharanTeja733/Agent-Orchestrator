## Compensation and Benefits Policy

### 1. Purpose
This policy outlines the compensation structure, benefits programs, and reward systems at Company Inc. Our philosophy is to offer competitive, equitable, and performance-aligned compensation that attracts and retains top talent.

### 2. Compensation Philosophy
Company Inc benchmarks compensation against the 65th percentile of the technology industry in our geographic markets. We believe in:
- Pay equity across gender, race, and other protected characteristics
- Rewarding high performance through differentiated compensation
- Transparency in compensation structure while maintaining individual privacy
- Regular market reviews to ensure ongoing competitiveness

### 3. Salary Structure

#### 3.1 Job Levels and Salary Bands
Each position is assigned to one of the following levels:
- L1 Associate / Junior Engineer: $50,000 - $75,000
- L2 Specialist / Engineer: $70,000 - $100,000
- L3 Senior Specialist / Senior Engineer: $95,000 - $140,000
- L4 Lead / Principal Engineer: $130,000 - $180,000
- L5 Manager / Senior Principal: $160,000 - $220,000
- L6 Director / Distinguished Engineer: $200,000 - $280,000
- L7 VP / Fellow: $260,000 - $350,000
- L8 C-Suite / Senior Fellow: $330,000 - $500,000

#### 3.2 Pay Schedule
- Salaries are paid bi-weekly (26 pay periods per year)
- Pay day is every other Friday
- Direct deposit is mandatory; physical checks incur a $10 processing fee
- Pay stubs are available electronically through the ADP portal

#### 3.3 Salary Reviews
- Annual merit review cycle occurs in January
- Adjustments are based on performance rating, market data, and internal equity
- Promotion-related increases are effective from the promotion date
- Off-cycle adjustments require VP and HR approval and are limited to retention and market adjustment cases

### 4. Variable Compensation

#### 4.1 Annual Performance Bonus
- L1-L3: Target bonus of 10% of base salary
- L4-L5: Target bonus of 15% of base salary
- L6-L7: Target bonus of 25% of base salary
- L8: Target bonus of 40% of base salary
- Actual payout ranges from 0% to 200% of target based on company performance and individual rating
- Bonuses are paid in February following the performance year

#### 4.2 Sales Commission
- Sales roles have a separate commission structure defined in individual comp plans
- Commission is paid quarterly based on closed revenue
- Accelerators apply above 100% quota attainment
- Commission disputes must be raised within 30 days of payment

#### 4.3 Equity Compensation
- Stock options (ISO for US employees, NSO for international) are granted at hire and annually
- Initial grant vests over 4 years: 25% after 1 year (cliff), then monthly thereafter
- Refresh grants are based on performance and retention considerations
- Exercise window for departing employees: 90 days from termination date

### 5. Benefits Programs

#### 5.1 Health Insurance
- Medical: Company covers 85% of premium for employees and 60% for dependents
- Dental: Company covers 100% of employee premium, 50% for dependents
- Vision: Company covers 100% of employee premium, 50% for dependents
- Enrollment is within 30 days of hire or during annual open enrollment (November)
- Coverage begins on the first day of the month following enrollment

#### 5.2 Retirement Plans
- 401(k) plan with company match: 100% of first 4% of employee contributions, 50% of next 2%
- Immediate vesting on employee contributions; 3-year graded vesting on employer match
- Roth 401(k) option available
- Annual contribution limits follow IRS guidelines

#### 5.3 Life and Disability Insurance
- Basic life insurance: 2x annual salary (company-paid)
- Supplemental life insurance: available at employee cost up to 5x salary
- Short-term disability: 60% of salary for up to 12 weeks
- Long-term disability: 60% of salary after 12 weeks, up to age 65

### 6. Additional Benefits

#### 6.1 Professional Development
- Annual learning stipend: $3,000 per employee
- Conference attendance: up to $2,500 per year with manager approval
- Certification exam fees reimbursed upon passing
- Internal training programs and mentorship opportunities

#### 6.2 Wellness Programs
- Gym membership reimbursement: up to $75 per month
- Mental health support: 12 free counseling sessions per year through EAP
- Annual wellness screening with $200 HSA contribution incentive

#### 6.3 Expense Reimbursement
- Business travel expenses reimbursed within 14 days of submission
- Home office stipend for remote employees: $1,000 one-time setup, $100 monthly
- Mobile phone allowance: $80 per month for eligible roles
